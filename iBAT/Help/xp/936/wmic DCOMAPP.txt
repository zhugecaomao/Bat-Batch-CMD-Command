
DCOMAPP - DCOM 程序管理。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

DCOMAPP ASSOC [<format specifier>]
DCOMAPP CREATE <assign list>
DCOMAPP DELETE
DCOMAPP GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
DCOMAPP LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
