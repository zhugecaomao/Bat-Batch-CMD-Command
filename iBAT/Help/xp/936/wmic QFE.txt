
QFE - 快速故障排除。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

QFE ASSOC [<format specifier>]
QFE CREATE <assign list>
QFE DELETE
QFE GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
QFE LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
