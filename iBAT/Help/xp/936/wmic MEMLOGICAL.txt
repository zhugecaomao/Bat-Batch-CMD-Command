
MEMLOGICAL - 系统内存管理 (配置布局和内存可用性)。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

MEMLOGICAL ASSOC [<format specifier>]
MEMLOGICAL CREATE <assign list>
MEMLOGICAL DELETE
MEMLOGICAL GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
MEMLOGICAL LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
