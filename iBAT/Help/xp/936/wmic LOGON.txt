
LOGON - 登录会话。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

LOGON ASSOC [<format specifier>]
LOGON CREATE <assign list>
LOGON DELETE
LOGON GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
LOGON LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
