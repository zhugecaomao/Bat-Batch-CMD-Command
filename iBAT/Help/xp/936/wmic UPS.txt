
UPS - 不可中断的电源供应 (UPS) 管理。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

UPS ASSOC [<format specifier>]
UPS CREATE <assign list>
UPS DELETE
UPS GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
UPS LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
