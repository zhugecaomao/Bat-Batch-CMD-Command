
PORT - I/O 端口管理。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

PORT ASSOC [<format specifier>]
PORT CREATE <assign list>
PORT DELETE
PORT GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
PORT LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
