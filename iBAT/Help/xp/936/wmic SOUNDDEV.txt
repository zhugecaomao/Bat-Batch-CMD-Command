
SOUNDDEV - 声音设备管理。

提示: BNF 的别名用法。
(<alias> [WMIObject] | <alias> [<path where>] | [<alias>] <path where>) [<verb clause>].

用法:

SOUNDDEV ASSOC [<format specifier>]
SOUNDDEV CREATE <assign list>
SOUNDDEV DELETE
SOUNDDEV GET [<property list>] [<get switches>]
SOUNDDEV LIST [<list format>] [<list switches>]
