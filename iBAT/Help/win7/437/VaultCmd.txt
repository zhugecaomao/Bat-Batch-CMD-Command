Creates, displays and deletes stored credentials.
Following commands are supported.Use VaultCmd /<command> /? for further help 
VaultCmd /create
VaultCmd /lock
VaultCmd /unlock
VaultCmd /load
VaultCmd /unload
VaultCmd /list
VaultCmd /listschema
VaultCmd /listcreds
VaultCmd /addcreds
VaultCmd /deletecreds
VaultCmd /listproperties
VaultCmd /setproperties
